<title>Investigating the relationship between schedules and knowledge transfer in software testing</title>

This empirical study investigates the relationship between schedules and knowledge transfer in software testing. In our exploratory survey, statistical analysis indicated that increased knowledge transfer between testing and earlier phases of software development was associated with testing schedule over-runs. 

<results>
	<result>
		A qualitative case study was conducted to interpret this result. 
		<TypeOfResult>Qualitative</TypeOfResult>
	</result>
	<result>

	<result>
